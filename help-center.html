Does a recipient need an account with U.S. Bank if I send money with Zelle?

An account with U.S. Bank isn't required to receive money with Zelle. The recipient will receive an email or text with instructions to claim the money you send.

We suggest letting them know you're using Zelle to send them money, before completing the transaction. This way, your recipient has the opportunity to ensure their account information on their Zelle profile is correct. If they're not enrolled with Zelle, the recipient needs to do so to collect the funds.

Contact a banker for more help.
